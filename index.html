What game modes are available?

We offer three main game modes:

  • Survival: Classic survival gameplay with an economy and shop.
  • Skyblock: Start on a floating island and expand your world. Includes an economy and shop.
  • Creative: A free-build environment where you can unleash your creativity.

Is there an economy system?

Yes! Our Survival and Skyblock servers feature a fully functional economy system. You can earn in-game currency by playing and trade with other players.

How do I access the shop?

You can access the in-game shop by typing /shop in the chat. The shop allows you to buy and sell items, blocks, and more.

Are there any rules?

Yes, we have a few simple rules to ensure everyone has a great time:

  • No griefing or stealing.
  • Be respectful to other players.
  • No hacking or cheating.
  • Follow the instructions of staff members.

How do I report a player or issue?

If you encounter any issues or need to report a player, please contact a staff member in-game or on our Discord server.
